Welcome to BlockAcademy!

BlockAcademy is an online classroom built on the Ethereum blockchain. Here, you can view all students in the classroom.

Features

  • View Students: Easily view all students in the classroom.
  • Secure: Built on the Ethereum blockchain for secure and transparent operations.
  • Easy to Use: Simple and intuitive interface.

About BlockAcademy

BlockAcademy is the main address of Education, Training, and Study! BlockAcademy is a cutting-edge online learning management system designed by Google to foster effective communication and collaboration between educators and students. Its primary objective is to simplify the sharing and delivery of learning resources, including course materials, assignments, surveys, and documents.
